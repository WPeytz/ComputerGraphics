About PCF (Percentage Closer Filtering)

PCF is a shadow antialiasing technique that softens shadow edges by averaging multiple shadow map lookups. Instead of sampling a single texel from the shadow map, PCF samples a 3×3 grid (9 samples) of texels around the fragment's shadow coordinate and averages the results.

This produces softer, more realistic shadows with reduced aliasing artifacts compared to single-sample shadow mapping. The trade-off is increased shader complexity and 9× more texture samples per fragment, but the visual improvement is significant.
